## Brief
Create a cinematic field portrait that highlights the precision of marine research while honoring the human scale of the crew.

## Concept
A moving expedition journal shot on the edge of the fjord. The camera stays close, framing breath, gloved hands, and cold spray as rhythm.

## Production
We planned a two-day remote unit with minimal footprint, capturing dawn, mid-day, and night sequences in one continuous run.

## Cinematography
Natural light is paired with controlled bounce to keep faces textured. The palette stays muted, with steel blues and soft whites.

## Edit Rhythm
Cuts are measured at the pace of the crew. Long takes anchor the narrative, while inserts of instruments create tempo.

## Color / Finishing
Color passes emphasize clean neutrals and deep water density. Highlights remain restrained to preserve the cold air.

## Outcome
A film that feels precise and human, aligned with the brand voice and the research team experience.
