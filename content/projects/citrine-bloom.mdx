## Brief
Create a fashion film that feels architectural and calm, allowing the garments to lead the sequence.

## Concept
The camera follows fabric in motion. Light falls in structured panels, framing the body as sculpture.

## Production
A controlled studio environment with a rotating key light to sculpt shape without shadow clutter.

## Cinematography
Long, clean lenses with minimal distortion. The camera glides on a track for controlled lateral movement.

## Edit Rhythm
Cuts are driven by fabric arcs and subtle gestures. The pacing feels intentional and minimal.

## Color / Finishing
Soft neutral grade with pale lilac highlights to echo the collection palette.

## Outcome
A refined visual asset deployed across digital lookbooks and launch events.
