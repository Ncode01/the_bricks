## Brief
Capture a night story with intimate texture and calm pacing.

## Concept
We follow a courier through a city in fog. Lighting feels practical, like a memory of streetlight.

## Production
Two nights on location, small crew, handheld and dolly passes with minimal footprint.

## Cinematography
Low-key lighting and slow shutter for soft trails. Highlights are diffused with mist.

## Edit Rhythm
Long, deliberate sequences with short pulses to mark narrative shifts.

## Color / Finishing
Cool shadows with restrained cyan accent for signage and reflections.

## Outcome
A short film used for festival submissions and brand storytelling pitches.
