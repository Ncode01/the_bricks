## Brief
Translate a live performance into a graphic sequence with bold lighting and mechanical rhythm.

## Concept
A studio performance captured through strong side light and silhouette. The edit punches with the chorus.

## Production
One-day studio shoot with a split lighting rig and multi-camera coverage for sync and detail passes.

## Cinematography
High contrast key with soft fill to preserve skin texture. Lighting cues define the beats.

## Edit Rhythm
Cuts align to percussive hits. Repetition builds tension before the chorus drop.

## Color / Finishing
Deep blacks with restrained saturation. Skin tones remain clean to avoid posterization.

## Outcome
A music video release supported by social cuts and performance stills.
