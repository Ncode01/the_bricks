Elvoir | Valentine's Day Campaign

The edit is where the emotion is actually built. For Elvoir's Valentine's Day campaign, we were brought in to establish the heartbeat of the piece entirely in post-production. Taking the raw visual assets, we focused on deliberate pacing, rhythmic cuts, and seamless transitions to craft an intimate, cinematic narrative. By meticulously shaping the flow of the footage, we transformed great standalone shots into a cohesive, high-converting digital asset that perfectly captured the romance and aesthetic of the brand.
