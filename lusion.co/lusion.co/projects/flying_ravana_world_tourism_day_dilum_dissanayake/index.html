Flying Ravana x Vella Group | World Tourism Day with Dilum Dissanayake

Talking about extreme adventure tourism requires a little more than just pretty mountain views — you need the visionary behind the drop. For World Tourism Day, we partnered with Vella Group of Companies to sit down with Dilum Dissanayake, the Head of Ravana Adventures. While the director guided the narrative, we were completely dialed in on the visual execution. Handling the documentary cinematography, we kept the framing intimate and engaging, pairing it with a razor-sharp video edit and a lush, vibrant color grade that brings the epic, sun-drenched backdrop of Ella to life. It's an insightful, high-altitude founder interview that proves Sri Lankan tourism isn't just surviving — it's soaring.
