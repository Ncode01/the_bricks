98 Acres x Vella Group | World Tourism Day with Thashinthan Panchanathan

How do you run the number one luxury mountain resort in Asia? You sit down with the guy who keeps the whole operation at the top of the game. For our second World Tourism Day feature with the Vella Group of Companies, we brought Thashinthan Panchanathan, General Manager of 98 Acres Resort & Spa, in front of the lens. While the director led the conversation on what makes Ella the ultimate luxury destination, we handled the heavy lifting visually. With our documentary cinematography, we perfectly framed the interview against the iconic, misty peaks of Little Adam's Peak. Paired with a seamless video edit and a rich, earthy color grade, we created an interview piece that feels as premium and expansive as the resort itself.
