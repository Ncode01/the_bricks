CFW Day 2 | Mikail Hameed - High Fashion Runway Film

Experience the cutting edge of Sri Lankan fashion with this exclusive runway film showcasing Mikail Hameed's collection at Colombo Fashion Week (CFW) Day 2. This high-end fashion cinematic video is a deep dive into avant-garde form, rigid garment structure, and a bold commitment to pushing contemporary style boundaries. Captured by The Bricks production team with the direction of Shameeme Ismail, our fashion cinematography emphasizes the intricate geometry and premium textiles of these made-to-order and made-to-measure designer pieces. Through precise video editing and a moody, editorial color grade, we elevated this runway showcase into a premium visual experience that matches the luxury and exclusivity of the collection.
