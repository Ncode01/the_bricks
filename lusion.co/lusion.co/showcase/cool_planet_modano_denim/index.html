Cool Planet | Modano Denim Collection

Raw texture, indigo dyes, and pure attitude. We hit the pavement to capture the rugged, street-style energy of the Modano Denim collection for Cool Planet Sri Lanka. For this high-impact piece of fashion commercial videography, it was all about the grit and movement — framing the textures, the fits, and the effortless, bold vibe of the collection in motion. It's sharp, dynamic, and proof that premium denim never goes out of style.
