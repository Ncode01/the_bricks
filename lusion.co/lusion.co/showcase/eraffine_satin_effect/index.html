e Raffiné by Sage & Olive | The Satin Effect

Slipping into something a little more cinematic. We partnered with e Raffiné to bring "The Satin Effect: For Moments Between Moments" to life. This piece of high-end fashion videography and editorial photography is all about capturing the quiet, luxurious drape of premium satin. With soft lighting, fluid camera movement, and effortless styling, we crafted a visually rich, scroll-stopping aesthetic that makes you feel the fabric right through the screen. It's pure, liquid elegance in motion, designed to elevate the brand's digital presence.
