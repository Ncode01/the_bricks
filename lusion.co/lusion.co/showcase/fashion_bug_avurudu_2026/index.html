Fashion Bug | Sinhala & Tamil New Year Campaign 2026

Celebrating the season with style and tradition. We partnered with Fashion Bug Sri Lanka to bring their festive vision to life for the Sinhala & Tamil New Year (Avurudu). This fashion campaign video blends cultural warmth with contemporary retail appeal, capturing the vibrant colors and joyous spirit of the season. Through thoughtful lighting design, dynamic commercial videography, and a rich, vibrant color grade, we elevated the festive narrative into a visually stunning, high-energy promotional piece. It's a joyful, scroll-stopping campaign designed to connect with audiences and drive seasonal excitement.
