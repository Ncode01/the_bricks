Jasper House Hiriketiya | Finding Balance: Lina's Yoga Journey

From courtroom chaos to coastal calm. For this captivating mini-documentary at Jasper House Hiriketiya, we sat down with resident yoga instructor Lina to capture her inspiring pivot from a high-stress legal career to finding her center on the mat. Handling the on-ground production, our documentary cinematography was designed to visually mirror her personal transformation. We paired intimate, emotionally grounded interview framing with smooth, breath-guided camera movements during her oceanfront yoga sessions. By contrasting the spoken intensity of her past with the serene, sun-drenched visual reality of her present, we crafted a deeply human storytelling piece that adds profound soul and authenticity to the property's wellness offerings.
