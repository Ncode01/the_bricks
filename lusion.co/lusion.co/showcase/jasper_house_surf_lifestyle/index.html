Jasper House Hiriketiya | Surf & Lifestyle Commercial

Golden hour glow, salty air, and pure coastal energy. Partnering with Digital 101 Agency, we stepped behind the lens to capture the ultimate Sri Lankan surf lifestyle for Jasper House Hiriketiya. Handling the cinematography, we chased the sun and the swell, focusing on dynamic movement and sun-drenched framing to translate the boutique hotel's laid-back luxury into a visceral visual experience. From the paddle-outs to the poolside downtime, our camera work was built to deliver raw, authentic travel videography that makes you want to pack a bag, grab a board, and head straight to the South Coast.
